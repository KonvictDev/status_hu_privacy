1. Information Collection and Use

The App's core functionalities require access to specific types of data on your device, which is handled exclusively locally. This data is never transmitted to me or any third parties.

  • Media Access (Status Saver Feature): The Status Saver feature requires access to your device's media storage. Specifically, it needs permission to read files from the WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business status folders (`/storage/emulated/0/Android/media/com.whatsapp/WhatsApp/Media/.Statuses`). This permission is a core requirement for the App's primary purpose: to display and allow you to save statuses.
  • Notification Access (Recover Messages Feature): The Recover Messages feature requires access to your device's notifications. I have built a Notification Listener service within the App to capture and save the content of incoming WhatsApp notifications. This data (sender name, message content, and timestamp) is stored locally on your device to enable you to view messages that have been deleted.
